INSTRUCTIONS

This game requires at least two players; one player as the defuser and at least one player as the manual expert(s).
The defuser is not allowed to read the manual and the manual expert(s) may not look at the bomb.

The defuser and manual expert(s) must communicate verbally and work together to solve and defuse the bomb.
The defuser is given 3 minutes and two strikes to defuse a randomized bomb before the bomb detonates.

The manual expert(s) may look over the manual before the defuser starts the game.
Communication may not start until the game is started.

Click THIS and make sure it is at an audible and comfortable volume.
